FILENAME PATTERNS
=================
Single media: {platform}_{author}_copas_io.{ext}
Multi media: {platform}_{author}_copas_io_{index}.{ext}
ZIP files: {platform}_{author}_copas_io.zip

Examples:
- twitter_rwhendry_copas_io.jpg (single image)
- twitter_sosmedkeras_copas_io.mp4 (single video)
- twitter_mikuroQ_copas_io_1.mp4 (first of multi-video)
- twitter_IndonesiaGaruda_copas_io.zip (zip for multi-image)
